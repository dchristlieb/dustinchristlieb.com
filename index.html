About Me

Hello! I'm Dustin Christlieb, a Sr. Cloud Architect at DoiT International. My passion lies in architecting robust cloud solutions and assisting administrators in navigating the complexities of their enterprise technology, particularly focusing on collaboration and productivity suites like Google Workspace.

I thrive on problem-solving and continuously seek innovative ways to enhance security, efficiency, and user experience within cloud environments.

My key areas of expertise include Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Google Workspace Administration, Cloud Security, Scripting (GAM, Apps Script), Identity Management (IAM), Governance Risk and Compliance (GRC), and Mobile Device Management (MDM).
